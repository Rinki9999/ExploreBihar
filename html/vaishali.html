Welcome to Vaishali

Vaishali, one of the world’s first republics, is a sacred and historic town in Bihar. It holds great importance for Buddhists, Jains, and historians alike.

🌸 Why Visit Vaishali?

  • Birthplace of Lord Mahavira (Jainism)
  • Lord Buddha preached his last sermon here
  • Home of ancient Lichchhavi republic
  • Famous Ashokan Pillar and Buddhist Stupa

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time due to pleasant weather and seasonal Buddhist/Jain festivals.

🛣 How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is in Patna (approx. 70 km)
  • By Train: Hajipur Junction is the nearest major railway station
  • By Road: Connected via NH-77 from Patna and Muzaffarpur

🏞 Places to Explore in Vaishali

  • 🗿 Ashokan Pillar
  • 🕌 Buddha Stupa I & II
  • 🌳 Abhishek Pushkarni (Coronation Tank)
  • 🛕 Vishwa Shanti Stupa
  • 🏯 Vaishali Museum
