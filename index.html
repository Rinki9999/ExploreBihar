Welcome to Bihar Darshan

Bihar – one of the most ancient and culturally rich states of India – is a land where history breathes through its monuments, and spirituality lives in every corner. From the sacred Bodh Gaya to the tranquil banks of Ganga, Bihar offers a soulful journey full of stories, wisdom, and beauty.

Pride of Bihar

Aryabhata

Renowned mathematician and astronomer credited with the invention of zero.

Champa Flower

The state flower of Bihar, symbolizing purity and beauty.

Veer Kunwar Singh

Legendary freedom fighter who led the 1857 revolt from Bihar.

Chandragupta Maurya

The warrior emperor from Bihar who founded the Maurya Empire.

Litti Chokha

Litti Chokha is a traditional dish of Bihar made with roasted wheat balls stuffed with spices, served with mashed vegetables. It represents the simplicity and earthy flavors of Bihari cuisine.

Chhath Puja

Chhath Puja is one of the most significant festivals of Bihar, dedicated to the Sun God. Celebrated with devotion and rituals along riverbanks, it showcases Bihar’s spiritual depth and cultural richness.

Madhubani Painting

Madhubani painting, also known as Mithila art, is a traditional folk art form from Bihar. Known for its vibrant colors and intricate patterns, it is created using fingers, twigs, and natural dyes — often depicting mythological themes.

Nalanda

Before Oxford or Harvard, Nalanda University (5th century) had over 10,000 students and 2,000 teachers from across Asia, with a massive library and structured education system.

Highlights of Bihar

Vaibhav Suryavanshi: IPL's Youngest Sensation

Bihar's 14-year-old Vaibhav Suryavanshi became the youngest player in IPL history, debuting for Rajasthan Royals in 2025.

Khelo India Youth Games 2025

Bihar hosted the 2025 edition of Khelo India Youth Games, featuring over 10,000 athletes across 28 sports in Patna, Rajgir, Gaya, Bhagalpur, and Begusarai.

Patna Metro Project

The Patna Metro project is underway, aiming to improve urban mobility and reduce traffic congestion in the city.

Bihar Budget 2025-26

The state budget focuses on infrastructure development, education, and healthcare, with significant allocations for rural areas.

Boost in Makhana Exports

Bihar's makhana exports have seen a surge, contributing significantly to the state's economy.

Education Initiatives in Bihar

New educational policies aim to improve literacy rates and provide quality education in rural areas.

Tourism Development in Bihar

Efforts are underway to promote Bihar's rich cultural heritage and historical sites to boost tourism.
